Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country located in southern Africa. It is known for its stunning landscapes, diverse wildlife, and iconic landmarks such as Victoria Falls, one of the largest waterfalls in the world. Zimbabwe is rich in cultural heritage and has a long history of ancient civilizations, including Great Zimbabwe, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
