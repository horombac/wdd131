Site Plan - The Laundry Lounge

Site Name

The Laundry Lounge

The name represents a welcoming and convenient space where customers can have their laundry cleaned with ease while relaxing.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about The Laundry Lounge's services, pricing, pickup and drop-off options, and contact details.

Scenarios

  • Where can I do my laundry near me?
  • How can I schedule a laundry pickup and delivery?
  • What are the pricing options for different laundry services?

Color Schema

  • Light Blue #90D5FF - Used for header and footer including some call to action buttons
  • Soft Grey #D2D2CF - Used for the background and content areas
  • Black - used for text

Typography

  • Arial - Used for body text.
  • Roboto - Used for headings.
